Welcome to Dr. Chen and Associates!

Welcome to our practice. Dr Chen and Associates is a quality dental practice in the historic Rotunda, conveniently located on West 40th Street, between Falls Road and University Parkway, just one minute from the Cold Spring Lane exit off the Jones Falls Expressway (I-83).

This Rotunda office has provided the community with high-quality dental care since 1974. We would welcome the opportunity to provide you and your family with quality dental care in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

In order to accomodate busy schedules, we have evening hours Monday and Wednesday. Please feel free to call us at (410) 235-8525 and schedule an appointment.

We look forward to hearing from you!
